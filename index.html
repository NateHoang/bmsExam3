Lecture 5: GI physiology Digestive Organs
Substance Regulatory Type Cell Type/Source Stimuli Inhibitors Primary Functions
Gastrin Endocrine G cells (Stomach Antrum) Stomach distention, amino acids, vagal stimulation (GRP) Hydrogen ion (low pH), Somatostatin Stimulates HCl secretion and growth of GI mucosa
CCK (Cholecystokinin) Endocrine/ Paracrine I cells (Duodenum) Fatty acids, monoglycerides, amino acids, and peptides - Stimulates pancreatic enzymes/gallbladder contraction; inhibits gastric emptying
Secretin Endocrine S cells (Duodenum) Low pH (acid) and fatty acids in duodenum - Stimulates pancreatic bicarbonate; inhibits gastric acid secretion
GIP (Glucose-dependent insulinotropic peptide) Endocrine K cells (Intestine) Fatty acids, amino acids, and oral glucose load - Stimulates insulin release; inhibits gastric HCl secretion
Motilin Endocrine Enteroendocrine cells - - Increases GI motility
Leptin Endocrine Adipocytes (Fat cells) - - Decreases appetite via anorexigenic neurons
Ghrelin Endocrine Stomach enteroendocrine cells Simple nutrients (fatty acids, glucose, hydrolyzed proteins) - Increases appetite via orexigenic neurons
Peptide YY Endocrine Intestine L-cells - - Inhibits gastric emptying, acid, motility, and food intake
GLP-1 Endocrine L cells (Intestine) - - Stimulates insulin secretion and epithelial proliferation
Somatostatin Paracrine D cells (GI tract/Pancreas) Hydrogen ion in stomach lumen Vagal stimulation Inhibits all GI hormones, gastric acid, and exocrine pancreas
Histamine Paracrine Mast cells/ECL cells Gastrin and vagal stimulation Somatostatin Increases gastric HCl secretion
Serotonin Paracrine Enteric neurons/ECL/Mast cells - - Regulates smooth muscle and water absorption
VIP (Vasoactive intestinal peptide) Neurocrine Mucosa/Smooth muscle neurons - - Relaxes smooth muscle; stimulates pancreatic bicarbonate
Enkephalins Neurocrine Nerves (Smooth muscle/mucosa) - - Slows GI motility; inhibits fluid/electrolyte secretion
GRP (Gastrin-releasing peptide) Neurocrine Vagus nerve Vagal stimulation - Stimulates gastrin release from G cells
